MTG Augur

A Scrfall Powered Clipboard

Why Not Use Scryfall?

Are you tired of split-screening a Scryfall window and your trusted deck editor, typing in cards one name at a time over and over and over again? With MTG Augur, you can easily explore card options and save all the cards you need with a click of a button! Then, when you're ready, effortlessly copy the list to your clipboard and reflect on the hours of tedious typing saved!

MTG Augur is made for only one purpose: a card clipboard for Magic card searches. Tailored for deck-building research, MTG Augur does not have the numerous features that Scryfall has.

How Does It Work?

1 Make your search using Scryfall's syntax

2 When you find a card you like, click it

3 View and copy your clipboard
